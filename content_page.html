Introduction

Poisonous substances in the ocean are a major threat to marine life. This section introduces the topic and its significance.

Types of Poison

Different types of poison affect marine creatures, including chemical pollutants, oil spills, and heavy metals.

Effects on Sea Animals

Exposure to toxins can lead to diseases, genetic mutations, and population decline in marine species.

Solutions

Preventive measures, strict regulations, and pollution control can help protect sea animals from harmful toxins.
